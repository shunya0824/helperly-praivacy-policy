About Helperly

Helperly is a platform that connects household service workers (helpers) with customers in Cebu, Philippines. We facilitate service bookings, payments, and communication between helpers and customers.

Getting Started

For Customers

Looking for household help? Follow these steps:

  • Download the Helperly app from the App Store
  • Create an account and select "Customer"
  • Complete your profile with your location and preferences
  • Browse available workers and their services
  • Book a service and make secure payments through the app

For Workers

Want to offer your services? Here's how:

  • Download the Helperly app from the App Store
  • Create an account and select "Worker"
  • Set up your profile with your experience and services
  • Set your availability and pricing
  • Start receiving booking requests from customers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download the app, select whether you're a customer or worker, and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your name, email, phone number, and address.
How do payments work?
All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Customers pay at the time of booking, and the platform fee (20%) is collected. The remaining amount is paid directly between parties.
What services are available?
Helperly offers various household services including cleaning, babysitting, cooking, laundry, shopping assistance, pet care, elderly care, driving, gardening, and tutoring.
How do I cancel a booking?
You can cancel a booking through the app. Go to your bookings, select the booking you want to cancel, and follow the cancellation process. Please review our cancellation policy for details on refunds.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, all payment information is processed and stored securely by Stripe, a PCI DSS compliant payment processor. Helperly does not store your credit card details.
How do I change my profile information?
Open the app, go to your profile section, and tap "Edit Profile." You can update your name, phone number, address, profile photo, and other information.
How do I delete my account?
You can request account deletion through the app settings. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your personal data will be anonymized within 90 days, though transaction records may be retained for legal compliance.
What languages does the app support?
Helperly supports English, Japanese, Tagalog, and Cebuano to serve our diverse community in Cebu.
Where is Helperly available?
Currently, Helperly operates in Cebu, Philippines, connecting local household service workers with customers in the area.

Account & Privacy

Your privacy and security are important to us. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure authentication with Supabase Auth
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies in our database
  • Regular security audits and updates

Safety Tips

For Customers:

  • Review worker profiles and ratings before booking
  • Communicate clearly about your service requirements
  • Use the in-app messaging system for all communications
  • Provide feedback and ratings after service completion

For Workers:

  • Keep your profile up-to-date with accurate information
  • Maintain clear communication with customers
  • Arrive on time for scheduled services
  • Follow professional standards and safety protocols
